📖 How to Play

🎮 Controls

WASD or Arrow Keys - Move

Mouse - Look around

Left Click - Shoot

Right Click - Switch weapons

ESC - Pause game / Exit pointer lock

🔫 Weapons

🔫 Assault Rifle: Single-shot precision weapon

⚡ Machine Gun: Rapid-fire suppression weapon

🧟 Zombies

🧟 Normal: Standard zombies (available from wave 1)

🏃‍♂️ Runner: Fast but weak (wave 2+)

🦾 Tank: Slow but tough (wave 4+)

👹 Brute: Large boss zombies (wave 6+)

💊 Health System

Collect health packs to restore 25 HP

Avoid zombie attacks to survive longer

Health packs spawn every 15 seconds

🌊 Wave System

Survive endless waves of increasingly difficult zombies

Each wave has more zombies and stronger types
